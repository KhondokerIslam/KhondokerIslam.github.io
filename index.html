About

I am a NLP Exchange Research Masters student affliated across University of Groningen and Saarland University. Recently, I was affliated with @GroNLP research group to analyze the reasoning capability of multilingual LLMs on complex QA[preprint]. During my Bachelor thesis, I demonstrated the limitation of mBERT during the encoder-only era [findings.of.emnlp.2021, aacl.main.2022].

Teaching

I am always looking for undergraduate/master students interested in doing their thesis on NLP. If you are interested in any of my research topics, please contact me as soon as possible, I try to supervise one student every semester. I also supervise Hiwis (research assistants), but this depends on projects availability.
