About

I am a NLP Master's student of the European Union Exchange Research Program, affiliated with both the University of Groningen and Saarland University. Recently, I worked with the @GroNLP research group to evaluate the reasoning capabilities of multilingual LLMs across various languages [1]. In my Bachelor's thesis, I demonstrated the limitations of mBERT during the encoder-only era[2],[3]. Additionally, I contributed to a Multilingual Multi-Modal lab, where I focused on enhancing the linguistic capabilities of vLMs in Hebrew, Dutch, and English to improve OCR for their respective historical documents[4].
