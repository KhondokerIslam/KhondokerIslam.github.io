About

I am a NLP Master's student of the European Union Dual Degree Research Program, affiliated with both University of Groningen and Saarland University. Recently, I worked with the @GroNLP research group to evaluate the reasoning capabilities of LLMs across various languages [1]. In my Bachelor's thesis, I demonstrated the limitations of encoder-only LMs.[2],[3]. In between, I enhanced the linguistic and visual capabilities of vLMs in ancient Hebrew, Dutch, and English to improve OCR for their respective historical documents[4]. Separately, I mapped tonal emotion values into textual representations to improve LM‚Äôs comprehension of audio features[5].
